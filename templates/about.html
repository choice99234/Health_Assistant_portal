Health Assist Portal

A comprehensive platform for managing your health

About

Like never before, machine learning has driven and revolutionized the world today. With its accurate and reliable results based on trained data, and use of algorithms for efficient predictions, it has helped in all aspects of human life. Specifically, the healthcare sector is one of the areas that needs to adopt these advanced machine learning techniques to ease and address complex issues.

"Health Assist Portal" is a versatile project based on machine learning that aims to empower people to effectively manage their health. This platform combines predictive analytics to predict diseases, recommend appropriate medications, provide detailed descriptions of diseases, offer necessary precautions, individualized nutrition plans, customized exercise programs based on symptoms, and more.

With voice recognition capabilities, users can easily input symptoms through speech, which is then converted to text for accurate predictions. A chatbot-enhanced portal ensures seamless communication by providing assistance and solutions to user queries. Additionally, the platform includes a segment that evaluates health insurance costs, taking into account factors such as age, gender, smoking status, region, and number of dependents.

Users can also access a repository of health information to promote awareness and understanding in healthcare. The "Health Assist Portal" embeds a machine learning prediction blog where users can read and search health-related data to stay well-informed and empowered.

Furthermore, the platform includes a diabetes prediction tool where people can check their diabetic status based on different indicators.
