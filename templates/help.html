Help Guide - Health Assist Portal

Login Instructions

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Health Assist Portal URL.
  2. Enter your username and password in the provided fields on the login page.
  3. Click on the "Login" button to access your account.

Using the Health Assist Portal

Dashboard Overview

Upon logging in, you will be directed to your personalized dashboard. Here, you can:

  • View personalized health insights and recommendations.
  • Access predictive analytics for disease prediction and medication recommendations.
  • Input symptoms either by typing or using voice recognition.
  • Interact with the chatbot for assistance and answers to health-related queries.
  • Explore health information and blogs for valuable insights.

Using Voice Recognition

To input symptoms using voice:

  1. Ensure your microphone is enabled and working properly.
  2. Click on the microphone icon in the symptom input field.
  3. Speak clearly into the microphone.
  4. Your spoken symptoms will be converted to text automatically.

Using the Chatbot

The chatbot is designed to provide instant assistance:

  1. Type your question or concern in the chat window.
  2. The chatbot will respond with relevant information or instructions.
  3. For complex queries, the chatbot may escalate the conversation to a human operator.

Managing Health Insurance Costs

You can evaluate health insurance costs using the portal:

  1. Navigate to the Insurance Cost Evaluation section.
  2. Enter your details such as age, gender, smoking status, region, and number of dependents.
  3. Receive a detailed cost estimation based on the provided information.

Additional Notes

For any technical issues or further assistance, please contact our support team at support@healthassistportal.com.
